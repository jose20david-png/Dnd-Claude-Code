
**Examples:**
- `python "C:\Users\jose2\Desktop\roll-dice-skill\scripts\roll.py" 20` — Roll a d20
- `python "C:\Users\jose2\Desktop\roll-dice-skill\scripts\roll.py" 20 advantage` — Roll with advantage
- `python "C:\Users\jose2\Desktop\roll-dice-skill\scripts\roll.py" 20 normal 3 --label "Perception Check"` — d20 + 3
