Campaign Timeline
DAY 1
Arrival in Phandalin
Rurik arrives via the Triboar Trail. Makes contact with Toblen Stonehill at the Stonehill Inn. First glimpse of Redbrand presence in town — locals intimidated and silent.
DAY 1
Redbrand Confrontation
Redbrands openly threatening civilians. Silga identified as field commander — half-elf, brutal, feared. Redbrand base traced to Tresendar Manor.
DAY 1–2
Sister Garaele & the Harper Warning
Sister Garaele reveals her Harper affiliation and shares intelligence: something ancient is stirring beneath Wave Echo Cave. She shows Rurik a Harper cipher describing a "Witness" sealed 400 years ago.
DAY 2–3
Wave Echo Cave Expedition
Rurik and company descend into Wave Echo Cave. Encounter evidence of the Mind Flayer's thrall network. Discover the Seal Chamber — a 15-foot circular seal with spreading cracks, the symbol of a watching eye at its center. The Witness is aware and pressing.
DAY 3
Artifacts Retrieved
All five artifacts recovered from their pedestals in Wave Echo Cave: crystal sphere, stone tablet (seal schematic), staff with pulsing gem (divine conduit), ceremonial dagger, mysterious encrypted book. Party withdraws before the Mind Flayer returns.
DAY 3
Glasstaff Unmasked — Iarno Freed
Iarno Albrek, the Redbrand wizard known as Glasstaff, was identified and brought in. Now recovering at the shrine, pale and silent. Former enemy, current uncertain asset.
DAY 3 — Evening
Party Assembles at the Shrine
Full party consolidates at the Shrine of Luck. Artifacts locked in the blessed catacombs. Harpers and Order of the Gauntlet alerted — ETA approximately 6 days. Qelline arrives with additional intel. Four former thrall prisoners recovered and present.
DAY 4 — Morning
Sister Garaele's Vision
Garaele recounts a vision from Selûne: the seal was breathing, the book glowed, and a binding ritual appeared — not to reinforce the seal, but to redirect the Witness inward. She warns true time remaining may be only 4–5 days, not 10–14. Silga's face appeared in the vision, suggesting the Mind Flayer has warned her.
DAY 4 — Morning
The Stormfather Speaks — Book Unlocked
Rurik places the book on the altar and prays. The Stormfather channels through him — stormlight erupts across the shrine, ancient words flow from Rurik's mouth unbidden. The magical cipher on pages 11–25 dissolves under divine will. The binding ritual structure is revealed: five voices, five elements, one anchor bound eternally to the seal. Rurik immediately volunteers to be the anchor.
DAY 4 — Morning
Five Voices Committed
Sildar, Gundren, Toblen, and Iarno each volunteer for the binding ritual without being asked. Rurik refuses to command any of them — all five step forward freely. The ritual requires all five at dawn at Wave Echo Cave's Seal Chamber.
DAY 4 — Afternoon
Silga Raids the Shrine
A large Redbrand force — 10–12 soldiers led by Silga herself — assaults the Shrine of Luck. The party escapes through the catacombs beneath the altar with all five artifacts and all personnel intact. Silga breaches the shrine moments after they descend. She now knows they escaped and will search the roads.
DAY 4 — Evening
Regrouped at Old Marta's Cabin
The full party reaches Old Marta's cabin safely. Marta confirms the binding ritual's history. Iarno begins decrypting the final pages (21–25). The ritual must be performed at Wave Echo Cave.
DAY 5
Scouting Missions
Rurik, Sildar, and Qelline scout the Harper waystone — confirmed safe and unknown to Redbrands. Rurik and Borik scout Wave Echo Cave — find 3 Redbrand scouts on the ridge, growing to 5 by evening. Silga is building a kill zone at the main approach.
DAY 6 — Morning
The Miners' Passage — Breeding Chamber Discovered
Party uses Gundren's miners' back entrance to bypass Silga's 5 scouts. Deep in the cave, they find a hidden chamber: 7 ceremorphosis pods, the Mind Flayer's illithid breeding army. Decision made — preserve the staff's power for the ritual. The pods will be dealt with on the way out.
DAY 6 — The Ritual
The Binding Ritual — Rurik Becomes the Bearer of the Seal
In the Seal Chamber, the obsidian seal cracked and failing from Mind Flayer corruption. Sister Garaele, Sildar, Gundren, and Iarno form the ring at the four cardinal points. Rurik stands at the center — the anchor. The Witness surges with everything it has. Rurik holds. The Stormfather stands behind him. The seal closes. Whole. Glowing steady. The Witness is CONTAINED. Rurik levels to Level 3 — Bearer of the Seal.
DAY 6 — After the Ritual
Breeding Chamber Destroyed
On the way out, Rurik casts Shatter into the breeding chamber — 15 points of thunder damage obliterates all 7 pods. The passage collapses under 100+ tons of rock, burying the Mind Flayer's army entirely. The party escapes through the miners' passage as the cave settles.
DAY 6 — Evening
Return to Marta's Cabin — Long Rest
Party returns to Marta's cabin. Rurik briefs Marta on the ritual. All allies present. Long rest completed. The connection to the Seal is constant — Rurik feels it like a second heartbeat. The Stormfather's presence is brighter than it has ever been.
DAY 7 — Morning (NOW)
Day 7 Begins — Planning the Next Move
Full party at Marta's cabin. Spell slots restored. Harpers ~2 days out. Silga still commands the Redbrands in Phandalin. The Mind Flayer is at large, cut off from the Witness. The next fight is above ground.
