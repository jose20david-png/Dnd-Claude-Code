Campaign Timeline
DAY 1
Arrival in Phandalin
Rurik arrives via the Triboar Trail. First glimpse of Redbrand presence — locals intimidated and silent. Contact made with Toblen Stonehill at the Stonehill Inn.
DAY 1
Redbrand Confrontation
Redbrands openly threatening civilians. Silga identified as field commander — half-elf, brutal, feared. Redbrand base traced to Tresendar Manor.
DAY 1–2
Sister Garaele & the Harper Warning
Sister Garaele reveals her Harper affiliation. A Harper cipher describes a "Witness" sealed 400 years ago — something ancient is stirring beneath Wave Echo Cave.
DAY 2–3
Wave Echo Cave Expedition
Rurik and company descend into Wave Echo Cave. Discover the Seal Chamber — a 15-foot circular seal with spreading cracks, a watching eye at its center. The Witness is aware and pressing.
DAY 3
Artifacts Retrieved & Glasstaff Unmasked
All five artifacts recovered. Iarno Albrek (Glasstaff) identified and freed — now an uncertain but valuable asset. Party consolidates at the Shrine of Luck.
DAY 4 — Morning
The Stormfather Speaks — Book Unlocked
Rurik places the book on the altar and prays. Stormlight erupts across the shrine. Ancient words flow unbidden. Pages 11–25 dissolve. The binding ritual is revealed: five voices, one anchor, bound eternally to the seal. Rurik immediately volunteers.
DAY 4 — Afternoon
Silga Raids the Shrine
10–12 Redbrands led by Silga assault the Shrine of Luck. The party escapes through the catacombs with all artifacts. Silga breaches the shrine moments after they descend.
DAY 4 — Evening
Regrouped at Old Marta's Cabin
Full party reaches Marta's cabin safely. Iarno begins decrypting final pages (21–25). The ritual must be performed at Wave Echo Cave.
DAY 5
Scouting Missions
Harper waystone confirmed safe. Wave Echo Cave scouted — 3 Redbrand scouts on the ridge, growing to 5 by evening. Silga is building a kill zone at the main approach.
DAY 6 — Morning
The Miners' Passage — Breeding Chamber Discovered
Party uses Gundren's back entrance to bypass Silga's scouts. Deep inside: a hidden chamber with 7 ceremorphosis pods — the Mind Flayer's illithid breeding army. Decision made: preserve the staff's power for the ritual.
DAY 6 — The Ritual
The Binding Ritual — Rurik Becomes the Bearer of the Seal
In the Seal Chamber, the obsidian seal cracking from Mind Flayer corruption. Garaele, Sildar, Gundren, and Iarno form the ring at the four cardinal points. Rurik stands at the center — THE anchor. The Witness surges. Rurik holds. The Stormfather stands behind him. The seal closes. Whole. Glowing steady. THE WITNESS IS CONTAINED. Rurik levels to Level 3.
DAY 6 — After the Ritual
Breeding Chamber Destroyed
On the way out, Rurik casts Shatter into the breeding chamber — 15 thunder damage obliterates all 7 pods. The passage collapses under 100+ tons of rock. The Mind Flayer's army is buried. The party escapes through the miners' passage.
DAY 7 — Morning (NOW)
Day 7 Begins — Planning the Next Move
Full party at Marta's cabin. Long rest complete. Full HP and spell slots. Harpers ~2 days out. Silga still commands the Redbrands in Phandalin. The Mind Flayer is at large. The next fight is above ground. Three escape routes on the table — Rurik's call.
