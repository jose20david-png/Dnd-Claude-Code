## Day 1 — Morning | 2026-05-21
*Character Creation*

The night unfolds with revelation and purpose. Kel and Sera settle into the common room of the remote mountain inn, where the weight of discovery presses heavily upon them. Through the pages of the ancient Netherese journal, they piece together a haunting history—two thousand years of desperate attempts to bind a coty, a consciousness vast beyond mortal comprehension, finally contained through a frequency resonance rather than force. But the binding degrades. The Netherese scholars' own entries betray their growing desperation, their failing attempts at reinforcement, until at last—silence. No answers about what the Spren truly is, only the confirmation that its containment is failing.

Kel's frustration at the journal's incompleteness transforms into insight. The real answers, he realizes, lie not in ancient texts but in direct observation—in the ruins themselves, in communion with the Spren. And as he speaks, a deeper realization crystallizes: the cosmic improbability of their meeting cannot be coincidence. The mathematics are astronomical. Something has woven their paths together at precisely this moment. Sera, who has fled the ruins in terror, begins to understand that running is no longer possible—nor is it the answer. There is a purpose in their convergence, mysterious as it may be.

As dawn breaks over the mountains, Kel and Sera stand ready to depart. The innkeeper has prepared provisions. Sera has dressed for the journey ahead, sleep having granted her some measure of peace and resolve. Kel, having spent the night in elven meditation, emerges centered and clear-eyed. In that golden morning light, they acknowledge each other not as strangers bound by circumstance, but as true partners—two seekers of knowledge about to walk into the unknown together. The ruins await. The Spren awaits. And with mutual respect and shared purpose, they step out into the day, ready for whatever wild and terrible truths await them in the high places of the world.
