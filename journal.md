# Rurik Stormhammer — Campaign Journal

---

## Day 1 — Morning | 2026-05-21
*Character Creation*

The night deepened as Kel and Sera delved into the mysteries contained within the ancient Netherese journal, uncovering centfailed attempts to bind an incomprehensible cosmic entity through the resonance of the Fabrial. The entries revealed a desperate history—from initial struggles twousand years ago, through eventual success with the Resonance Sphere, to the terrifying realization that the binf was degrading faster with each passing decade. The final entry, penned three hundred years prior, offered only resignation and silence. Yet within this absence of answers, Kel's brilliant mind seized upon something far more significant: the astronomical improbability of their meeting in this remote inn was no coincidence. The convergence of events—Sera finding the journal, which led to the fabrial, which led back to Kel—spoke to something deeper, something meaningful that neither could yet comprehend. As Kel rambled through calculations of cosmic probability, blushingly clarifying that his observation held no romantic implication, Sera found herself drawn not away from this mystery, but toward it. She agreed to stay, to guide him through the ruins she knew intimately, to face whatever awaited them at the source. That night, while Sera sought fitful sleep, Kel meditated by the dying fire, his elven mind drifting through labyrinths of theory and observation, crystallizing a single certainty: he must see the Spren itself, must witness directly what no theory or ancient text could fully explain. As dawn broke golden over the mountains, they stood together—two souls unlikely to have ever met, now bound by cosmic probability and a shared purpose. With provisions gathered and resolve hardened, they stepped toward the door, ready to face whatever the ruins and the awakening Spren would reveal.
